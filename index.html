About This Dashboard

The Santa Clara University Waste Collection Dashboard provides an interactive visualization of waste disposal data for different buildings on campus. Users can explore and compare the amount of waste generated by each building, the distribution of waste streams (recycling, compost, landfill), and the accuracy of waste stream categorization. The dashboard allows users to select specific dates for analysis and offers insights into waste management practices. With its intuitive charts and interactive features, the dashboard facilitates data-driven decision-making and supports ongoing monitoring of waste collection efforts, promoting sustainability at Santa Clara University.
